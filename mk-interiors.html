About

Five years, one way of working.

Kiran M founded MK Interiors after five years designing the rooms people actually spend their lives in — kitchens that get cooked in every day, living rooms that survive kids, guests, and quiet Tuesday evenings alike.

The studio takes on a small number of residential projects at a time, from a single room refresh to a full layout rework, with the same attention either way: how the space is used first, how it looks second.
